Accent System · Chakra & Elementthe accent colours a motif & border — the illustration stays, the hue changes

In Talisman a themed class simply re-inks the symbol and its frame in the chakra or element colour. The drawing is constant; the colour tells you what today is about.

The talisman seal

An original hand-built emblem — sun, lotus and crescent moon inside a worded ring. Drawn as line-art so it can be stamped, embroidered, foiled or tattooed. The centre motif is what gets re-inked for chakra/element themes.
