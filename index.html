Brand Mark

The stamp mark

A bold monogram — MY — pressed like a stamp into the space. The rectangular frame signals craft and precision. At scale it's a graphic block; reduced, it's a mark that reads on every surface from tote bags to IG profiles.

Brand Mark

The sun mark

A folk-modern solar symbol — ancient archetype made graphic and clean. The concentric circle system anchors the eye; the rays signal energy and movement. Works in two colours, one colour, stamped or engraved.
